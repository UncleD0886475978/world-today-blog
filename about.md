## About The World Today

**The World Today** is a daily current affairs blog covering geopolitics, conflict, and history — published automatically every day.

Each day, three posts are generated covering:

- **Geopolitics** — Major world events, diplomatic developments, and power shifts
- **Conflict & Security** — Active wars, military developments, and security analysis
- **History & Analysis** — Historical context and parallels to today's events

*Updated automatically three times daily at 7 AM, 1 PM, and 7 PM UTC.*
