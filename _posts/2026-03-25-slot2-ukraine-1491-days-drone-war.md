**Kyiv — March 25, 2026**

Russian forces launched over 200 combat engagements per day in recent weeks — a pace Ukrainian military chief Oleksandr Syrskyi described as evidence Moscow is preparing a broader offensive push.

## The Drone-Dominated Battlefield

The skies over eastern Ukraine are thick with unmanned aerial vehicles. Ukrainian forces rely on drones to break up Russian mechanized assaults, while Russia deployed over 9,000 kamikaze drones in a single 24-hour period. Movement across the front is nearly impossible without drone cover.

## Ukraine Strikes Deep Into Russia

Ukrainian forces struck the Novatek Ust-Luga oil terminal in Leningrad Oblast — the second such strike in three days — and hit a Russian shipbuilding plant. About 40% of Russia's oil export capacity has been halted, the worst supply disruption in modern Russian history.

## The Diplomatic Dimension

President Zelensky warned the Iran conflict is drawing Western attention from Ukraine. "Our diplomatic meetings are constantly postponed. There is one reason: war in Iran," he told the BBC. "For Putin, a long war in Iran is a plus" — rising oil prices directly benefit Russia's war economy.

After 1,491 days, the war shows no signs of ending.
