**Kyiv — March 26, 2026**

Ukraine's Armed Forces struck a Bastion coastal missile system in Crimea overnight, destroying launch platforms used to fire Russia's Zircon hypersonic missiles — a significant blow to Russia's ability to threaten Black Sea naval assets.

## The Day's Battlefield Picture

Ukrainian defenders repelled 158 combat engagements in 24 hours. Russia launched 70 airstrikes dropping 231 guided aerial bombs and 9,414 kamikaze drones. Russian losses: 1,210 personnel killed, 49 artillery systems, and 2,038 UAVs destroyed.

## Kharkiv Goes Underground

One of the war's most remarkable stories is Kharkiv's transformation into an underground city. Located just 40 kilometres from the Russian border and regularly targeted, authorities built an elaborate subterranean infrastructure of shelters, schools, and municipal services over two years. The city Russia expected to capture in weeks has not only survived — it has adapted.

## Back-Channel Signals

A Russian delegation headed by Vyacheslav Nikonov — grandson of Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov — is set to meet US officials in Washington, suggesting back-channel communication beyond the public posturing of both sides.

Day 1,492. The war continues. And so does Ukraine.
